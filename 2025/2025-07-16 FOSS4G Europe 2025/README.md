* Minghini M., Escriu J. and Kotsev A. (2025) Monitoring the FAIRnessof geospatial data: Lessons learnt from the European Union. _FOSS4G Europe 2025_, July 14-20, 2025 [[pdf](FOSS4GE_AT_MarcoMinghini.pdf)]
